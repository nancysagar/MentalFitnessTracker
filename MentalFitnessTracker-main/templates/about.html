About Mental Fitness Tracker

The Mental Fitness Tracker is an innovative web application developed as part of a comprehensive degree project aimed at empowering users to monitor and enhance their mental well-being effectively.

In today's fast-paced world, prioritizing mental health is paramount. Our platform offers a user-friendly interface designed to facilitate tracking various aspects of mental fitness, providing invaluable insights into personal well-being.

Utilizing advanced machine learning regression models, our application predicts users' mental fitness scores by analyzing a combination of numerical data and textual input. These predictions offer actionable insights, empowering individuals to make positive lifestyle changes for improved mental wellness.

While the Mental Fitness Tracker is a valuable tool, it is essential to emphasize that it does not replace professional mental health guidance. If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health issues, we strongly advise seeking support from qualified mental health professionals.

We are committed to fostering mental health awareness and providing individuals with the tools to take control of their well-being. Your interest in our project is greatly appreciated, and we welcome any feedback or inquiries you may have. Please feel free to reach out to us with your thoughts and questions.

Thank you for choosing the Mental Fitness Tracker. Together, let's prioritize mental wellness and embark on a journey towards a healthier mind.
